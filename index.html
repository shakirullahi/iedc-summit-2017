What is IEDC Summit ?

The Innovation Entrepreneurship development centres are small incubators physically available at Engineering, Polytechnic, Management and Arts & Science colleges which promotes Innovation and entrepreneurship from the college level. Kerala Startup Mission is supporting 193 IEDC across the state which helps the students to come up with innovative ideas and work within the IEDC and start their entrepreneurial journey. The summit is scheduled with two sets of panel discussions, on the first half we are trying to bring in two levels of entrepreneurs, one who have succeeded from Kerala and established their business across the globe and the young generation entrepreneurs who are building their enterprise globally.
